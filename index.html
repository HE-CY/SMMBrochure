Restaurants & Cafés

Fastest close if you lead with visibility, offers and consistency.

  • Sell lunch deals, brunch, events, delivery and seasonal specials.
  • Best package to push: Growth €300.
  • What matters: frequent posts, story-style visuals, reels, menu promos.

Beauty, Salons & Aesthetics

Clients buy trust, presentation and booking demand.

  • Use before/after, treatment education, reviews and clean branding.
  • Best package to push: Growth €300 or Premium €500.

Real Estate

Higher-ticket clients can support stronger pricing.

  • Sell listings, local area lifestyle, credibility and lead generation.

Hotels, Villas & Tourism

Visual businesses need content that drives booking intent.

  • Sell atmosphere, amenities, experiences and trust.

Gyms & Coaches

They buy membership growth, class visibility and social proof.

  • Use transformations, class clips, trainer tips and offer posts.
  • Best package to push: Growth €300.

Professional Services

Law, finance, consulting, clinics and B2B need authority first.

  • Sell credibility, consistency and premium presentation.
